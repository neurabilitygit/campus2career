Rising Senior customer brief

Career readiness intelligence for families before it is too late to adjust.

Rising Senior helps students and parents understand whether a student's college path is building credible evidence for a specific job after graduation. The system connects school, major, documents, uploaded transcripts, market data, target roles, and AI guidance into a practical view of readiness and next-best actions.

The problem it solves

Families often discover too late that a major, transcript, resume, or internship history does not clearly support the career outcome the student wants. Rising Senior is designed to make those gaps visible early, while the student can still choose better coursework, projects, relationships, and recruiting actions.

What the system does

  • Captures a student's college, major, minor, graduation timing, target job, and preferred geography.
  • Loads or extracts curriculum requirements from school catalogs and uploaded official PDFs.
  • Accepts transcripts, resumes, program requirement PDFs, and other evidence documents.
  • Maps student evidence against role requirements, market signals, skills, and degree progress.
  • Produces a readiness score, risk list, academic progress view, and recommended next actions.
  • Provides parent-friendly monthly briefs and student-facing scenario guidance.

Who benefits

Students See what evidence is missing, which actions matter most, and how different target roles change the readiness picture.
Parents Get a clear, non-technical view of trajectory, risk, and constructive questions to ask without micromanaging.
Coaches and advisors Review market mappings, student context, and the evidence behind recommendations.
Schools and partners Can eventually connect advising, career services, and recruiting signals into one more actionable layer.

Expected benefits

  • Earlier detection of weak alignment between coursework and career goals.
  • More focused student action plans based on evidence, not vague advice.
  • Better parent conversations because the system translates risks into practical questions and support actions.
  • Clearer use of uploaded transcripts, resumes, projects, and official school requirements.
  • Improved confidence before recruiting cycles, internship deadlines, and graduation decisions.

Why it is different

Rising Senior is not just a resume checker or college-major quiz. It is an evidence engine that joins student context, official curriculum, labor-market requirements, target-role intelligence, and AI-generated guidance. The value is in connecting these pieces into a live picture that can change when the student changes schools, majors, roles, or evidence.

Current MVP focus

The current product is focused on proving the core loop: student profile, school and major selection, source document upload, role scoring, parent dashboard, and AI-assisted guidance. The next stage is hardening data quality, improving role and curriculum coverage, and making the user journey simpler for non-technical users.
